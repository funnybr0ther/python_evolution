jumpFrequency:Frequency at which the zombie jumps
gravity:Degree at which the zombie is affected by gravity
jumpForce:Force of the jump
jumpAngle:Angle at which the zombie jumps
boredomTime:Time it takes for the zombie to get bored and enter in "boredom" state
boredomDuration:Duration of boredom state
spottingRange:Radius of the zombie's sight (circle), affects both food and mating sight
matingTreshold:Energy treshold above which the zombie stops looking for food and starts looking for another zombie in the same state
energyPercentage:Percentage of the zombie's energy that will me transfered to the child(promille)
